CCPA Opt-Out

Last Updated: November 12, 2023

WG Holdings Corp. values your privacy and recognizes the importance of your personal information. In compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents are entitled to request the exclusion of their personal information from any sale by our company. We are committed to ensuring your privacy rights.

How to Opt Out:

To opt out of the sale of your personal information, please follow these steps:

• Contact Us: Reach out to our support team via email at privacy@joinyara.com
• Verification Process: To protect your data, we will verify your identity through our secure process. Please be prepared to provide information necessary for verification.
• Confirm Opt-Out: Once your identity is verified, we will process your request to opt out and confirm that your personal information has not been sold.

Additional Information:

If you have any questions about how we handle your personal information or your privacy rights, please visit our [Privacy Policy]https://joinyara.com/privacy-policy-new page. For further assistance, you can contact us directly at privacy@joinyara.com.


Note to California Residents:
Yara does not sell personal information in the traditional sense. However, we respect and understand that you may still wish to ensure your personal information is not sold. Therefore, we provide you with the opportunity to opt out as per CCPA guidelines.
