Highest Standard Compounds
High Quality. Made in USA.

At Yara we manufacture all of our compound drugs in sunny Delray Beach Florida. Due to our 503B Federal compound status we must maintain the highest standards of compound pharmaceuticals.

Both Trizepatide and Semaglutide are on the drug shortage list which allows Yara, as a 503B, to produce compounds with the same active ingredient as Mounjaro®, Zepbound® & Ozempic® for minus the big pharma mark up.

Many pop-up weight loss sites are purchasing weight loss drugs from 503A compound manufacturers who have less stringent compliance or FDA approval.

We don't know about you...but when injecting ourselves with drugs we want to make sure it's the most pure, regulated substances available!

Federally Regulated.

Our partner is referred to as a 503B outsourcing facility because they are required to register with the FDA pursuant to Section 503B of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.  

Outsourcing facilities, for example, are able to supply the healthcare system with compounded medications when the commercially available options are on the FDA’s Drug Shortages list.

Yara able to order compounded medications in bulk. This prescription-free, bulk ordering of compounded medications has made it easier, safer, and more affordable to have important medications readily available for patients.
