WHY DOES YARA OFFER A GUARANTEE?

Yara offers a 6-month money-back guarantee because we believe weight management is a long-term process. The 6-month mark, if the program has been followed, is a pivotal moment where long-term results are showing. As we truly believe in our approach to Health Repair, we want to give you the time to work through the program, which is why we’ve chosen a 6-month guarantee.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE FOR THE MONEY-BACK GUARANTEE?

In order to qualify for the 6-month money-back guarantee, members must meet the following requirements:

Have joined the program after July 31st 2022
Be a qualifying member for a minimum of 180 days since sign up
Upload your medical form & labwork within 5 days of joining the program
Take the medication every week as prescribed, and not have requested a refill in your 5th or 6th month of membership
Track your weight in at least 21 of the first 26 weeks of your membership
Have lost less than 5% of your starting body weight, with a starting BMI of 30 of above

This offer is not available to:

Returning members
Members who cancel before the end of their first 180 days (6 months) of membership
Members who joined the program before July 31st 2022
Medication payments. This program covers the core Membership payment(s), not extra Medication payments.
WHEN CAN I REQUEST MY MONEY-BACK GUARANTEE?

Members may request a refund for the 6-month guarantee in month 7, between 181 - 210 days after signup, please send an email to help@joinyara.com with your refund request
