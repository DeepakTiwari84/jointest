How GLP-1s Work

Genetic, biological, and environmental factors can often make weight loss more difficult. GLP-1s can:

Appetite Regulation.

GLP-1s target brain appetite centers, decreasing hunger and increasing satiety, aiding in calorie reduction and a healthier diet.

Feel Fuller Longer.

GLP-1s delay the rate at which the stomach empties, which leaves you feeling full for longer than you would with natural GLP-1 levels.

Create Healthier Habits.

With GLP-1 meds managing appetite and cravings you can focus on changing your eating and exercise leading to long term behavioral change.

Federally Regulated.

Our partner is referred to as a 503B outsourcing facility because they are required to register with the FDA pursuant to Section 503B of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.  

Outsourcing facilities, for example, are able to supply the healthcare system with compounded medications when the commercially available options are on the FDA’s Drug Shortages list.

Yara able to order compounded medications in bulk. This prescription-free, bulk ordering of compounded medications has made it easier, safer, and more affordable to have important medications readily available for patients.
